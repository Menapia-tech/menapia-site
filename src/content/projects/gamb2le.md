Menapia is a research collaborator within the GAMB2LE programme, funded by ARIA. The project focuses on understanding and improving the representation of atmospheric boundary-layer processes that are critical to weather forecast accuracy.

Our contribution centres on deploying vertically profiling drone networks to collect calibrated atmospheric columns in support of the programme's research objectives.
