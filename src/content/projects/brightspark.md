Menapia is a research collaborator within the BRIGHTSPARK programme, funded by ARIA. This project explores how improved atmospheric observations can enhance solar energy forecasting and support the integration of renewable energy sources into the grid.

Our role involves deploying vertically profiling drone systems to capture atmospheric conditions relevant to solar irradiance forecasting.
