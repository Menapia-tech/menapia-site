Menapia operates as a subcontractor within the OTTER programme, funded by ARIA. The project investigates optimal strategies for deploying atmospheric observing systems to maximise forecast value.

We provide routine vertical profile observations from our multicopter drone network, delivering calibrated atmospheric columns that support the programme's experimental objectives.
