Menapia is a research collaborator within the DOOC project — Drone Observation of Clouds and their role in weather and climate — funded by UKRI and led by the University of Leeds.

The project uses drone-based atmospheric profiling to collect in-situ observations of cloud properties, contributing to improved understanding of cloud processes and their representation in numerical weather prediction and climate models.
